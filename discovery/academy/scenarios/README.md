# Academy Scenarios

Scenarios exist to challenge the current operational model.

They are not requirements.
They are not documentation.

A scenario is a piece of evidence that either strengthens or weakens
our current understanding of how the business operates.

The objective is not to prove the model correct.

The objective is to discover where the model is incomplete.

Every scenario follows the same structure:

1. Purpose
2. Current operational model
3. Timeline
4. Observation mapping
5. What did not fit?
6. Questions raised
7. Verdict
8. Model changes

Only repeated evidence is promoted into documentation.
